Data Rights Roundtable

October 19, 2021 - 12:00pm ET (Zoom)

Exploring how to streamline consumer data rights requests under CCPA

Please join us for a preview of the Data Rights Protocol (DRP). DRP is a draft specification that encodes a set of standardized request/response data flows for exercising Personal Data Rights provided under regulations like the California Consumer Privacy Act.

We are convening a discussion on how privacy compliance companies and covered businesses can implement DRP on a voluntary basis, and to provide a forum where stakeholders can pose feedback, questions, and ideas on the draft specification and its potential future directions. Topics include:

  • Scope of the Data Rights Protocol
  • The Role of Authorized Agents and DSR Providers
  • Issues and Options for Consumer Verification

Contributors to the DRP specification currently include Consumer Reports Digital Lab, MIT, privacy compliance tech providers DataGrail, Ethyca, OneTrust, Transcend, and WireWheel, covered businesses like Spokeo, and authorized agents like Mine and Surfshark.
